# 2Market Data Analytics Project

## Overview
Analysis of 2,216 customer records across eight countries for 2Market, a global 
supermarket. The project followed the CRISP-DM framework to answer three business 
questions: who are 2Market's customers, which ad channels drive the most conversions, 
and which product categories generate the most revenue?

## Tools Used
- **Excel** — data cleaning and preparation (SUBSTITUTE, AVERAGEIF, AVERAGEIFS)
- **SQL (PostgreSQL)** — cross-table queries joining customer and ad data
- **Tableau** — interactive dashboard for non-technical stakeholders

## Key Findings
- Spain was the highest spending country at £659,557 — nearly half of total revenue 
across all markets. The Middle East recorded the lowest at £3,122
- The 35–49 age group is the most commercially valuable demographic, spending the 
most across every product category
- Liquor was the top performing product by a significant margin, with non-vegetarian 
products consistently second across all demographics
- Twitter and Bulkmail were the most effective advertising channels; brochure ads 
performed least well
